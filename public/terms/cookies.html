COOKIE POLICY
Last updated September 28, 2025

This Cookie Policy explains how Aplii. (“Company”, “we”, “us”, and “our”) uses cookies and similar technologies when you use our app at https://app.aplii.ai (“App”).

What are cookies?
Cookies are small data files placed on your device. We only use cookies that are strictly necessary for authentication, security, and core functionality of the App.

Why do we use cookies?
We use first-party and third-party essential cookies solely to:
  • Authenticate users and maintain secure sessions (via Clerk).
  • Protect the App from abuse and bots, and ensure reliable performance (via Cloudflare).
We do not use analytics, advertising, or social media tracking cookies.

How can I control cookies?
Because we only use essential cookies for authentication and security, there is no in-app opt-out. You can control cookies at the browser level (e.g., block or delete), but doing so may prevent you from signing in or using secure areas of the App.
